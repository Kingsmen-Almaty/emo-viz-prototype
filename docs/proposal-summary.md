## AI Trail Entrance Direction

The proposal positions the AI Trail as the start of enhanced AI learning in ACSM. The entrance should introduce the AI Trail with a panel and mascot, feel eye-catching and futuristic, and include a simple AI-based interaction that demonstrates a practical application of AI.

The previous Prompt Lab placement at the entrance is deprioritised because of space and visitor-flow constraints, and because the robotic arm should remain the main lobby highlight.

## AI Wall Concept

The proposed AI Wall interaction is an AI facial sentiment analysis lighting installation. The visitor is prompted:

> How do you feel about the future of AI?

The visitor looks into the camera and reacts naturally. The system analyses the face and estimates expression / sentiment. Public-facing sentiment labels translate raw emotion categories into more approachable meanings:

| Detected expression | Visitor-facing sentiment | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Happy / Joy | Excited | Hopeful, positive, enthusiastic |
| Surprise | Curious | Amazed, intrigued, open to possibility |
| Neutral | Thoughtful | Calm, reflective, undecided |
| Sadness | Concerned | Worried about impact, ethics, society |
| Fear | Anxious | Uneasy, cautious, uncertain |
| Anger | Resistant | Frustrated, opposed, uncomfortable |
| Disgust | Distrustful | Skeptical, rejecting, distrustful |

## Proposed User Flow

1. Ambient attraction: camera scans passively and screen shows the AI future prompt.
2. Enter detection zone: visitor stands in front of the camera and face tracking starts.
3. Face lock: landmarks are detected and confidence is checked.
4. Sentiment analysis: visitor holds expression for 2-3 seconds while the system smooths results.
5. Sentiment mapping: the dominant expression maps to a visitor-facing sentiment.
6. Spatial reaction: LEDs shift into the matching colour pattern.
7. Hold result: sentiment remains visible for 5-8 seconds.
8. Exit / reset: when face detection is lost, screen and LED state fade back to idle.

## LED Colour Direction

| Sentiment | Colour direction | Pattern behaviour |
|---|---|---|
| Excited | Warm amber / gold / soft white | Broad glowing bloom, slow outward wash |
| Curious | Cyan / magenta / electric blue | Soft expanding colour pulse |
| Thoughtful | Soft white / cool blue | Slow breathing gradient |
| Concerned | Muted blue / desaturated cyan | Slow downward fade or dim wave |
| Anxious | Violet / pale cyan / cold blue | Uneven soft shimmer, low-intensity pulse |
| Resistant | Crimson / deep orange / red | Heavy slow pulse, dense colour wash |
| Distrustful | Green / amber / dim orange | Broken soft patches, uneven fading |
